What is a Dictatorship

Dictatorship is when you have one person who makes all decisions for the people. They speak and act for the people. They will in most cases limit basic rights and control propaganda to prevent people from wanting a new form of government.

Pros:

  • Things can get done fast/put into action
  • Can adpat to new Environments on the fly
  • Very simple
  • people have a reason to move up

Cons:

  • can lead to bad outcome if the wrong person is given power

How to handle the con of a dictatorship?

The con can be easily avoided by just inspecting the person who is picked to become dictator, don’t let someone who's like let’s kill everyone into power and it can become a very effective form of government. Just look at Rome where they would have dictators when the empire went into peril.
