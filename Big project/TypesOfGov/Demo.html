What is a Democracy

Democracy is a form of government where the majority makes the decisions, and everyone has an equal say in what happens. Most of the time they will elect people to speak for them and manage the laws and rules which is known as a representative democracy. example: USA

Read more about Democracy

Pros:

  • Everyone gets a say in what happens
  • There’s a reason to move up in the world
  • It’s the most fair type of government
  • Most of the time people have some rights

Cons:

  • The majority might make bad decisions
  • Can take a long time to have progress
  • Can take a lot of bureaucracy

What makes a dictatorship better?

A dictatorship doesn’t have these issues on top of being able to provide a reason to move up/work, it allow can allow for basic rights and eliminates the majority’s say by placing it all on one person.
