What is Communism

Communism is a type of government where everyone owns everything, private ownership is non-existent, and believes that the working people will just be selflessly devoted to their work for the good of the people. Decision are based on what is best for the community and not part of the community. example: China

Read more about the history of Communism

Pros:

  • Everyone owns everything
  • most people will have their needs provided for
  • everyone is equal(they all own the same stuff)

Cons:

  • There is no purpose to achive higher thinking/innovation(outside of it benfiting the community)
  • lots of bureaucracy
  • very slow to make progress

What makes a dictatorship better?

The main problem with communism is its lack of purpose for the everyday man, and how complex it is which leads to it being very inefficient. Whereas, a Dictatorship can still provide the needs of everyone and keep that incentive to keep improving by allow for private ownership of wants and/or using needs to get the comman man to work in exchange for these needs being provided.
